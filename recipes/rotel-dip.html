Rotel Dip

Super savory and easy rotel dip for any gameday.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Rotel(crushed tomatoes)
    • Block of cheese
  • Taco Seasoning(optional)

Steps

  1. Cook and strain ground beef
  2. Pour crushed tomatoes and chopped cheese into a crockpot aling with your taco seasoning
  3. Stir frequently and let heese melt
  4. Mix in ground beef
  5. Enjoy!
