BBQ Sausage Rice

Fast and cheap meal bbq sauage for any late night cravings

Ingredients

  • Smoked Sausage
  • Butter
  • BBQ Sauce
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Cook rice
  2. Cut sausage and sear with butter til desired texture
  3. Pour BBQ sauce into sausage andleave to simmer
  4. Pour mixture onto a bed of rice
  5. Enjoy!
